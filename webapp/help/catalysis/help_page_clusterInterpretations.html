Cluster interpretations

On the previous page, you made observations about patterns in your data. For each observation, you entered multiple interpretations about what that observation might mean (to reasonable people who happen to disagree).

On this page you will draw your interpretations together into meaningful groups. Clustering your interpretations creates a meaningful structure to your catalysis report. People will be able to look at your report at a high level (the perspectives), then "drill down" to the details that lie behind each perspective.

What to do here

First choose a catalysis report to work on. Then click the "Copy interpretations to clustering diagram" button. Move the interpretations around, placing like with like.

When you are satisfied that all of your interpretations are in groups that make sense, name the groups. For each group, click "New cluster." Give the cluster a name and description. Once you have named and described all of your clusters (now called perspectives), you are ready to print your catalysis report.

Frequently-asked questions
Do the interpretations have to be inside the cluster bubble to be counted as being associated with that cluster?

No. NarraFirma calculates the distance between the center point of each interpretation and the center point of each cluster. As long as an interpretation is closer to one cluster center than another, it doesn't matter if it's inside the cluster "bubble."

What if an interpretation is not in any cluster?

It will still go into the report, in the section of the perspective whose center it is closest to. If you don't want an interpretation in the report, go back to the previous page and delete it.

What if I cluster my interpretations, then add some more observations, then come back and cluster again?

If you click "Copy interpretations to clustering diagram" after you've already done it once, NarraFirma will copy over any new interpretation names it finds. It will not copy names that are already in the diagram. However, if you rename an interpretation, NarraFirma will copy over the new name, leaving the old name there. You will have to remove it yourself.
