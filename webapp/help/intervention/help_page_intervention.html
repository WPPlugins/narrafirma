Intervention

The intervention phase of a PNI project is a time in which action is taken based on sensemaking. Some examples of narrative interventions are storysharing spaces, narrative orientations, narrative mentoring networks, and participatory theatre events. Intervention serves to enhance the return of stories to the community, not to control or arrest it. Ideally, interventions grounded in effective narrative sensemaking produce positive change, sparking a spiral of storytelling that leads naturally into future projects.
