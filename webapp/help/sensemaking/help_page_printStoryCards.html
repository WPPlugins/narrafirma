Print story cards

On this page you can print story cards to be used in sensemaking sessions.

What to do here

Choose a story collection, then click the Print Story Cards button. The cards will appear in a separate window, and you can print them from your browser.

Frequently-asked questions
Can I format my story cards before I print them?

Yes, but you need to do that outside of NarraFirma. Instead of printing the cards, save the display as an HTML page. Then open that page in a word processor and format it as you like.
