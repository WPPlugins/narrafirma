Collection

In the collection phase of a PNI project, community members tell stories around a topic of concern, and those stories are collected in some way. The nature of collection can vary widely, from twenty stories gathered and used in an hour-long workshop to a thousand stories gathered over a year and used for many years afterward. But in one way or another, all PNI projects start with stories.

Frequently-asked questions
What kinds of story collection does NarraFirma support?

You can collect stories over the internet, or you can collect them in any other way and enter them yourself (as if you were responding over the internet).
