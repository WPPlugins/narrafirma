Respond to requests for post-project support

This page is meant to help you record any requests you get for support as your project winds down. Making a list of requests can help you remember to fulfill them, and it can help you keep good records for the next project.

What to do here

Every time somebody asks you for something, or to do something, related to your project, make a note of it here.
